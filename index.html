About Swastik Mart

Welcome to Swastik Mart - your trusted neighborhood grocery store since 2004. For over 20 years, we have been proudly serving the Indore community with quality products at honest prices.

Located at Kanak Smart City, Front of Aurobindo Hospital, 382, Shankheshwar Crossroad, nearby Hanuman Mandir, Bhawrasla, Indore, Madhya Pradesh 452001, we are committed to providing fresh groceries and daily essentials to local families. Swastik Mart has grown to become a household name in the area.

From premium rice and fresh spices to daily household needs, we carefully select every product to ensure the best quality for your family. Our commitment to freshness, quality, and affordability has made us the preferred choice for thousands of families.
